VGC Rulesets

VGC changes rulesets every once in a while.
The rulesets for Pokemon Scarlet and Violet
are listed below in order of that they were
used with a link to a website that talks
about the rules for each. Some knowledge to know
before looking at the rules is what a sub legendary,
restricted legendary, mythical, and paradox pokemon is.
Restricted Legendary: These are some of the most powerful Pokemon of all time. Norrmally found on the box art of each game. Norrmally not legal but when legal you are only allowed one or two. Sub Legendary: Any legendary that is not restricted. Allowed in quite a few rulesets
Mythical: Im not really sure what the difference between legendary and mythical is, but all you need to know is that they are almost never legal.
Paradox Pokemon: They are past and future forms of preexising Pokemon, many of them are quite strong are legal most of the time.

Smogon Tiers

Smogon is divided in many different tiers based
on how often a Pokemon is used and how powerful
it is. When you are playing in a specific tier you
can use the Pokemon in the tier you are playing in
, and the Pokemon in the tiers below it. For example
If you are playing in the OU tier you can use anything below OU and in OU
The tiers will be listed below in order along with
a short description of what each means.

  1. Anything Goes(also called AG), Insanely broken, breaks the other tiers(including Ubers!) in a bad way.
  2. Ubers, most strong legendaries go here, but being a legendary does not auto qualify for this. Some insanely broken normal mons go here.
  3. OU(OverUsed), Most strong normal mons go here
  4. UU(UnderUsed), A lot of these mons are still strong and relevent in OU, but are a bit weaker
  5. RU(RarelyUsed), Still a bit weaker but rarely seen in OU
  6. NU(NeverUsed), Very few are ever seen in OU
  7. PU(It stinks), Almost never seen
  8. ZU(ZeroUsage), Self-Expanatory

BTW there are also "in-between" tiers(e.g., UUBL(UU Banlist) being too strong for UU but too weak for OU)
I didnt mention them because they arent very relevent and wont come up very often.
