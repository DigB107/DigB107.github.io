Smogon vs VGC

Their are two different versions of
competitive Pokemon. Smogon, which is the fanmade singles version.
And VGC(Virtual Game Championship), which is the
official doubles version. I mostly play VGC but if I
ever get bored of it I switch to Smogon. Because I norrmally play VGC
I will be going more in depth with VGC.

Where I play

You might assume that most people play competitive Pokemon on their switches
, but most people play Pokemon on their mobile devices! Thanks to a
website known as Pokemon Showdown! It is a battle simulator
that is very game accurate. Pokemon Showdown was meant for Smogon, but
also supports VGC.

Vocab

  • Restricted Legendary: Super powerful mons that are norrmally not legal, but when they are you can only use one or two.
  • Sub-Legendary: Available in quite a few rulesets. These are all the legendaries that are not restricted.
  • Mythical: Just memorize which mons are legendary and which are mythical. Mythical Pokemon are almost never legal.
  • Paradox Pokemon: Pokemon that are past and future forms are preexisting Pokemon such as Flutter Mane(Misdreavus) Iron Hands(Hariyama). They are norrmally quite strong

Smogon Tiers

Smogon is divided in many different tiers based
on how often a Pokemon is used and how powerful
it is. When you are playing in a specific tier you
can use the Pokemon in the tier you are playing in
, and the Pokemon in the tiers below it. For example
If you are playing in the OU tier you can use anything below OU and in OU
The tiers will be listed below in order along with
a short description of what each means.

  1. Anything Goes(also called AG), Insanely broken, breaks the other tiers(including Ubers!) in a bad way.
  2. Ubers, most strong legendaries go here, but being a legendary does not auto qualify for this. Some insanely broken normal mons go here.
  3. OU(OverUsed), Most strong normal mons go here
  4. UU(UnderUsed), A lot of these mons are still strong and relevent in OU, but are a bit weaker
  5. RU(RarelyUsed), Still a bit weaker but rarely seen in OU
  6. NU(NeverUsed), Very few are ever seen in OU
  7. PU(It stinks), Almost never seen
  8. ZU(ZeroUsage), Self-Expanatory

BTW there are also "in-between" tiers(e.g., UUBL(UU Banlist) being too strong for UU but too weak for OU)
I didnt mention them because they arent very relevent and wont come up very often.
